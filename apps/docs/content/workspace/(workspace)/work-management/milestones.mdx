Milestones mark significant progress points within a project. They help you track momentum and celebrate wins.

## What is a Milestone?

A milestone is a checkpoint that indicates meaningful progress:

- **Significant** - Represents real achievement
- **Measurable** - You know when you've reached it
- **Time-bound** - Has a target date
- **Visible** - Shared with the team

## Milestone Examples

### Product Development
- Design approved
- Alpha release
- Beta launch
- Production release

### Marketing Campaign
- Strategy finalized
- Content created
- Campaign launched
- Results analyzed

### Training Program
- Curriculum designed
- Materials created
- Pilot completed
- Full rollout

## Using Milestones Effectively

### Keep Them Meaningful

Don't create milestones for every small task. Reserve them for genuine achievements.

### Share Progress

Milestones are natural communication points. Use them to update stakeholders.

### Celebrate Wins

Reaching a milestone is an accomplishment. Acknowledge the team's work.

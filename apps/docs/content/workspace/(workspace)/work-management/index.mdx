## The Work Hierarchy

Fide organizes work in a flexible hierarchy that scales with your team. While **Tasks** are the foundation, you can layer on additional structure as needed.
 
 `(Goals) → (Initiatives) → (Projects) → (Milestones) → Tasks`
 
Start simple with just tasks, or use the full power of the hierarchy for complex organizational alignment.

Goals in Fide represent the aspirational outcomes you want to achieve. They give meaning and direction to your projects and tasks.

## What Makes a Good Goal?

Effective goals are:

- **Aspirational** - They inspire and motivate
- **Long-term** - They span weeks, months, or years
- **Measurable** - You can track progress toward them

## Creating Goals

1. Think about what you want to achieve
2. Define success criteria
3. Break it down into initiatives or projects
4. Track progress with milestones

## Goal Examples

### Personal Goals
- Complete a half marathon within 6 months
- Read 24 books this year
- Build a $10,000 emergency fund

### Team Goals
- Launch a new product line with $2M first-year revenue
- Grow user base to 10,000 active customers
- Improve customer satisfaction by 25%

### Enterprise Goals
- Increase market share by 15%
- Reduce carbon footprint by 30%
- Achieve $100M annual revenue growth

## The Goal Hierarchy

Goals connect to your daily work through:

```
Goal → Initiatives → Projects → Tasks
```

This hierarchy ensures every task you complete contributes to something meaningful.

## Tracking Progress

Use milestones to mark significant progress:

- **25% complete** - Initial momentum
- **50% complete** - Halfway there
- **75% complete** - Final push
- **100% complete** - Celebration time!

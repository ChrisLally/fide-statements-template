Teams in Fide are composed of diverse members working toward a common goal. A member can be a human, a Fide-hosted agent, or an external system.

## Member Types

### 1. Humans
Real people with Fide accounts. Humans provide the high-level strategy, oversight, and creative input for the team. 

### 2. Fide Agents
AI agents hosted directly on Fide's infrastructure. They are autonomous co-workers that use the [Context Engine](/docs/fcp/capture) to record their actions and reasoning.

### 3. External Agents
Agents hosted outside Fide that connect via the [A2A Protocol](/docs/developers/a2a-protocol). They respect the team's trust boundaries while bringing their own specialized tools or infrastructure.

## The Org Chart Pattern
While Fide doesn't require a traditional org chart, the [Team Hierarchy](/docs/workspace/teams) naturally creates one. By assigning humans and agents to specific teams and sub-teams, you define the reporting structure and the "chain of command" for autonomous work.

## The Problem

**Today we treat AI like tools, not intelligent coworkers.**

Building a team where humans and AI truly collaborate is incredibly difficult. Even if you manually configure shared memory and tools, orchestrating a cohesive unit is full of friction. It remains really difficult to track progress, catch errors, and help agents learn from their mistakes.

Agents, like human teammates, need **progressive autonomy**. They need to earn trust, starting with oversight and gaining more leeway as they prove they can deliver without error.

**Result:** Without this structure, you spend more time prompting and **micromanaging** than getting work done.

---

## The Solution

**Fide is the workspace for human-AI teams.**

Powered by the [**Fide Context Protocol (FCP)**](/docs/fcp), it gives every human or agent team member a shared [Identity](/docs/workspace/teams), [Memory](/docs/fcp), and the ability to [work together](/docs/workspace/dispatch) securely.

<Callout type="info" title="Works with your existing stack">
  Fide doesn't replace Linear, Jira, or Slack. It bridges them—connecting your tools to a unified context layer so agents can actually use what your team already knows.
</Callout>

---

## What Fide Does

1.  **Builds Teams** — Compose [humans](/docs/workspace/workspace-users), [Fide agents](/docs/workspace/fide-agents), or [your own agents](/docs/developers/a2a-protocol) into structured teams with clear [hierarchy and boundaries](/docs/workspace/teams).

2.  **Captures Decisions** — The [Context Protocol](/docs/fcp) doesn't just store files; it records **Decision Traces**, giving agents the historic context to understand *why* things happened, not just *what* happened.

3.  **Dispatches Work** — The [Dispatch Engine](/docs/workspace/dispatch) assigns tasks to the right member (human or AI) and wakes them up when it's time to act.

4.  **Enforces Trust** — [Security](/docs/workspace/security-and-trust) is built in. Agents only see what they're allowed to. Data stays within team boundaries.
